The narrative of green energy and EVs is, therefore, one of undeniable progress and strategic repositioning. We are witnessing record growth and significant geopolitical shifts, with nations like China leveraging their preparedness. Yet, the journey is not without its ethical dilemmas and policy challenges. Moving forward requires not just technological innovation and investment, but also meticulous attention to ethical supply chains and smart, equitable public policy to ensure a truly sustainable and just energy future for all.
