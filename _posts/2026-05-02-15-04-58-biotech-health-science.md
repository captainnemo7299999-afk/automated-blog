Even lifestyle and consumer goods are feeling the impact, as evidenced by "Biotech Beauty Partnerships." This expansion into varied niches underscores the pervasive influence of biotechnological advancements.

Warnings from biomedical leaders about "catastrophic fallout" from proposed research cuts highlight the critical role of stable funding and supportive policy environments.
